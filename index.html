Feature Engineering - What Makes Songs Hard to Play


10-fold cross-validated logistic regression to classify the music notations into difficulty levels

The main advantage is interpretable results


Features

  • Chords used, i.e., C, G, D
  • Number of chords
  • Speed the song is played at
  • Position of notes on the fretboard
